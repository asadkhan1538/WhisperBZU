Definition

The word mathematics is derived from the Greek word mathematikos, which means "inclined to learn." Literally speaking, to be mathematical is to be curious, open-minded, and interested in always learning more!

Three Perspectives

  • As the sum of its branches
  • As a way to model the world
  • As a language
